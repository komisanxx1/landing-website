Company History

Ooviam Facades established in 2010 has emerged as a leading Manufacturer, Wholesaler and Retailer of Roofing System. Our wide range of products includes Tensile Structures Roofing, Membrane Tensile Structures, Tensile Fabric Structure, Tensile Roof Structure, Conical Tensile Membrane structure, Tensile car parking structures, Powder Coated Sheets etc. Compared to traditional construction, modular buildings are ready for occupation faster and easy to expand, reduce, relocate or remove. Conveyed under the course of able workers, these introduced items are broadly acclaimed and acknowledged for their preciseness, viability, and unwavering quality. Moreover, we offer the most efficient products at very competitive prices to various industries. Our ethical business practices and customer-centric approach has won us accolades from our esteemed clientele.
